BRABRAND

RIVER

(01)

BENCH

Located just a 10-minute walk from the nearest bus stop, this scenic bench offers stunning, unobstructed views of the Brabrand River. It’s a peaceful spot, ideal for enjoying sunsets or quiet moments, especially during colder months when the area is less crowded. While the bench is comfortable for short stays, it lacks a backrest and shade, making it less ideal for extended use or in bad weather.
