Just a 20-minute walk from the bus stop, Bellevue Beach offers serene views of the Nordic Sea, surrounded by sand and minimal greenery. A single bench provides seating but is weathered and less comfortable. The path is slippery and muddy due to construction, making access challenging. Popular with walkers and runners, the spot is peaceful and ideal for enjoying nature. It’s likely busier in summer, perfect for swimming and sunbathing.
